001 — About

Hi — I'm Ellie,
nice to meet you.

I'm a visual communication design student at Eastern Washington University with a lifelong obsession with video games. Not just playing them — understanding why they feel the way they feel, why some worlds pull you in and others don't, and how design is the invisible hand behind all of it.

Everything I make is an extension of me. My work has personality because I have personality — I don't know how to design something I don't care about, and I think that comes through. When I set my mind to something, I see it through. When something's hard, I stay curious instead of walking away.

I love seeing what other people make as much as I love making things myself. Design is more fun when it's a conversation. When I'm offline: thrifting, antiquing, spiraling through Pinterest, playing whatever game has my brain hostage this week.
