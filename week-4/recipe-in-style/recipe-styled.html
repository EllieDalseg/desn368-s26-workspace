Ellie's homemade brownies

This brownie recipe is perfect for anyone craving rich, fudgy chocolate brownies that come together quickly with simple ingredients, and can be ready within 30 minutes!


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oil or 1 stick (1/2 cup) melted butter
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Optional Add-Ins:

  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease small baking pan (8×8).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine oil or melted butter, cocoa and sugar. Mix until smooth.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, and mix until combined.
  4. Add flour and salt, and mix until combined.
  5. Spread batter in greased or lined pan.
  6. Bake at 350F for 18-20 minutes, until toothpick inserted in center returns clean.
⭐️ Tip: If toothpick is doughy, put the brownies in the oven for another 2-3 min. Make sure to keep an eye on them!

Recipe can also be doubled and baked in a 13×9 large baking dish.
